About me

A freelance web developer with a background in customer service

I am a fullstack freelance web developer in the Auburn area. Within my seven years of customer service experience I have had the privilege to build connections within my community by organizing projects to help businesses meet their sales goals.

After spending years "inspiring and nurturing the human spirit" at Starbucks, I started noticing a trend with the integration of tech-apps into businesses- they weren't considering their workers experience before building the apps. This led to confusion among workers and customers and congestion in production channels. So, in my free time, I started learning full-stack web development and reaching out to small businesses in my community, hoping to make positive changes to their customer and worker experience.

Education

I am currently in school at Arizona State University studying Organizational Leadership, so I can hone my skills as a community and team leader.

Starbucks

I spent three years as a supervising leader at Starbucks. My experience taught me time management, inventory management, and customer satisfaction models that I transfer into my career as a software developer.

100DEVS
