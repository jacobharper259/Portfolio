About me

A freelance web developer with a background in customer service

I am a fullstack freelance web developer in Atlanta. Within my seven years of customer service experience I have had the privilege to build connections within my community by organizing projects to help businesses meet their sales goals.

After spending years "inspiring and nurturing the human spirit" at Starbucks, I started noticing a trend with the integration of tech-apps into businesses; it felt like they weren't fully considering their workers experience before building their apps. This led to confusion from workers to customers, and congestion in production channels. So, in my free time, I started learning full-stack web development and reaching out to small businesses in my community, hoping to make positive changes to their customer and worker experience. I spent 6 months developing a web application to help starbucks baristas hone their craft and gain proficiency in the POS layout.

After gaining a personally surprising amount of knowledge in frontend and backend Javascript development, I began helping others within the 100DEVS community with all things webdev from explaining how to center a div to giving lectures on MVC and OOP.

ASU

I went Arizona State University - Online For two years. School is not for everyone. I achieved the results I wanted by being a community taught developer

Starbucks

I spent three years as a supervising leader at Starbucks. My experience taught me time management, inventory management, and customer satisfaction models that I transfer into my career as a software developer.

100DEVS

I had the privilege of joining the 100DEVS team in early 2022. I've helped new coders learn HTML, CSS, and Javascript by providing step by step advice throughout their coding journey.
