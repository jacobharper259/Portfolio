Projects

Landing Page Generator

This internal tool automates the creation of landing pages for over 40 dealerships, ensuring fast updates, boosting SEO, and maintaining consistency when new car models are released. It has significantly reduced manual coding and increased operational efficiency across all dealerships.

GA4 Analytics Visualization Program

This tool transforms Google Analytics 4 data into dynamic and interactive visualizations, making it easier for businesses to make data-driven decisions and optimize their digital marketing strategies. The program allows for in-depth analysis and reporting in a user-friendly interface.

The Barista Trainer

Designed to train Starbucks baristas on POS systems, this tool uses AI and real-world scenarios to simulate customer orders. It helps baristas gain proficiency, and includes a random customer generator that generates over 8.12 × 1022 unique "orders."

Custom URL Shortener for Email Marketing

This custom URL shortener was created to boost the effectiveness of web email marketing campaigns. By generating branded, shortened URLs, it improves link trustworthiness, bypasses spam filters, and enhances both deliverability and engagement, making it easier for emails to reach and engage the intended audience.

Automation Plugin Suite for CRM and CMS

This custom Automation Plugin Suite was designed to streamline workflows across CRM, CMS, and project management tools, automating repetitive tasks and synchronizing data. It improves operational efficiency and enhances team coordination, allowing teams to focus on strategic work instead of manual processes.
